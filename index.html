About Me

I've always been interested by the realm of engineering, whether it's mechanical, electrical, chemical, or any other branch. The numerous processes and layers of complexity involved in designing and developing solutions to real-world situations piqued my interest. I was astounded at how many meticulously planned and executed stages could come together to achieve even the most basic of objectives. This passion grew stronger over time as I learned more about how things work, from the circuits in a device to the mechanics of a car engine. Today, I am thrilled to be pursuing my interest as an Electrical Engineering major at the University of New Orleans. Every day, I get to deepen my comprehension of the principles that piqued my interest as a child, which is quite rewarding. Aside from my academic endeavors, I spend a large portion of my leisure time studying computer technology, which I find equally intriguing. Programming has been a creative outlet for me, allowing me to experiment, invent, and put ideas into action. Together, these passions motivate my desire to contribute to the technical world and explore the limitless possibilities it provides.
